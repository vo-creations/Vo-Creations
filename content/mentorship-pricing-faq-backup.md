## FAQ Section

1. **Do I need followers?**
   No. We create fresh accounts for campaigns. Your following doesn't matter — learning the format system does.

2. **What's the time commitment?**
   ~10 hours/week in Month 1 (training). 1-2 hours/day during campaigns. Works alongside school or a part-time job.

3. **Is the $500 campaign guaranteed?**
   Complete the training, demonstrate quality content, and you get placed on a real brand campaign earning $500 in Month 2. It's earned, not handed out.

4. **What if it doesn't work out?**
   That's why the program is 2 months, not 4 years. You invest $3K to find out fast. If the creator economy isn't for you, you'll know before committing more. Most creators earn their investment back within their first 2-3 agency campaigns.

5. **How is this different from creator courses?**
   Courses sell lessons and leave you alone. We put you on a real paid campaign in Month 2 and connect you to an active network with ongoing brand deals.

6. **Can I do this under 18?**
   Yes. Several creators started in high school. You'll need a parent/guardian for payment, but training and campaigns work the same.

7. **What do I need to start?**
   A phone with a decent camera, wifi, and ~10 hours/week. No equipment, no editing software, no experience.

8. **What happens after 2 months?**
   You join the creator network with access to ongoing brand deals, weekly coaching, and the creator community. Top creators earn $3-5K/month.

9. **What will I actually learn?**
   Talking-head storytelling, format testing, platform mechanics (TikTok, IG, YouTube, Facebook), brand brief execution, and how to go viral on purpose — not by accident.
